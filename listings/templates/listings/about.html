About iRent ProSpace

Welcome to iRent ProSpace — Kenya’s fastest-growing rental platform built to connect landlords and tenants in a transparent, stress-free way.

Our Mission

We simplify house-hunting for tenants and help landlords fill vacant units quickly, without expensive brokers or long vacancy periods.

What We Offer

  • Verified property listings with photos and clear pricing.
  • Direct chat / call / WhatsApp with landlords.
  • Smart filters: location, budget, bedrooms, amenities.
  • Free listing for landlords — no hidden charges.
